Homemade Enchilada

Homemade enchiladas have a bit of spiciness and kick to them and are also full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Tomatos
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chicken
  • Oil
  • Sour Cream
  • Lettuce
  • Queso Fresco

Steps

  1. Put chicken to boil with onion and garlic.
  2. Boil tomatos with garlic and onion.
  3. Shred lettuce and cruble Queso Fresco cheese.
  4. Blend boiled tomatos with onion and garlic.
  5. Shred chicken.
  6. Saute tomato sauce.
  7. Microwave tortillas to soften.
  8. Dip tortilla in tomato sauce.
  9. Fill with chicken and roll.
  10. Top with shredded lettuce and crumbled queso fresco cheese. And add a dollop of sour cream.
