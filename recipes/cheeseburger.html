Cheeseburgers

Charbroiled cheeseburger with American Cheese

A proper cheeseburger should be grilled on a BBQ and should be nice and juicy on a soft bun.

Ingredients

  • Hamburger meat
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Buns
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Grill burger patties on BBQ until cooked to desire.
  2. Add cheddar or american cheese on patty just before taking them off the grill so it has a chance to melt.
  3. Put buns on grill to toast for a minute or so.
  4. Spread desired condiments on buns.
  5. Top burger off with lettuce, tomato, onions, and pickles.
