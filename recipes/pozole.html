Pozole

Homemade Pozole

Pozole is a delicious soup full of flavors. It can be made with either chicken or beef.

Ingredients

  • Hominy
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chile Gujillo
  • Oregano
  • Cabbage
  • Raddish
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Boil chicken with garlic, onion, and salt.
  2. Boil chili with garlic, onion, and salt.
  3. Shred cabbage and slice raddish.
  4. Add hominy to boiling chicken.
  5. Blend chili with garlic, onion, and salt.
  6. Add blended ingredients to chicken and hominy.
  7. Serve and top off with cabbage, raddish, and oregano.
